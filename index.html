About

I'm Chao

I believe computers are created to create.

My journey with < code /> started about three years ago when I was asked to create an event marketing email. The experience of creating a template that looked 3000 times better than the default turned on my curiosity and interest in the world of coding. As I continued to dive deeper into it, I found myself sitting among 38 other of my brilliant peers inside the HackerYou classroom. That small spark of curiosity grew, and every minute I am amazed by the possibilities of what code can do. I’m always curious about finding better, smarter and sweeter solutions to a coding problem, learning new frameworks and turning ideas into code. I’m excited about the possibilities of creating art through codes using modern CSS and Javascript.
